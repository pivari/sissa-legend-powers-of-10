# Sissa's Legend & Powers of 10

An interactive STEM experience exploring the staggering power of exponential growth, from the ancient Indian Legend of Sissa to the vast scales of the universe and the heart of the atom.

## 🚀 Features
- **Interactive Storytelling**: Experience the "Wheat and Chessboard" problem firsthand through an immersive digital board.
- **Dual Scale Journey**: 
    - **Macro**: Travel from a single grain to quantities exceeding the known universe's capacity.
    - **Micro**: Zoom from a grain of rice down to the fundamental subatomic particles.
- **Customizable Narration**: Integrated Text-to-Speech (TTS) with **dynamic voice selection**. Users can choose their preferred "reader" from available system voices.
- **PWA Ready**: Installable on mobile devices as a Progressive Web App with full offline support via Service Workers.
- **Multilingual Support**: Available in English and Italian, with automated voice filtering based on the selected language.

## 🛠️ Built With
- **HTML5 / CSS3**: Featuring custom animations (Deep Space Rocket, Atomic Tunnel) and a responsive `vmin` design for cross-device compatibility.
- **Vanilla JavaScript**: High-precision mathematical logic using `BigInt` to handle astronomical integers (up to $2^{64}-1$) without precision loss.
- **Web Speech API**: Real-time narration of story milestones and scientific unit transitions.
- **Service Workers**: Advanced caching strategy for instant loading and offline educational use.
