# Sissa's Legend & Powers of 10

An interactive STEM experience exploring the staggering power of exponential growth, from the ancient Indian Legend of Sissa to the vast scales of the universe and the heart of the atom.

## 🚀 Features (v1.2 Update)
- **Interactive Storytelling**: Experience the "Wheat and Chessboard" problem firsthand through an immersive digital board.
- **Dual Scale Journey**: 
    - **Macro**: Travel from a single grain to quantities exceeding the known universe's capacity.
    - **Micro**: Zoom from a grain of rice down to the fundamental subatomic particles.
- **Visual Milestones**: Enhanced feedback with a **Centralized Icon System**. Icons represent physical volumes (e.g., a 50L sack for 1 million grains, a commercial truck for 1 billion).
- **Native Sharing**: Integrated **Web Share API** allowing users to share their mathematical discoveries directly to social media or messaging apps.
- **Customizable Narration**: Integrated Text-to-Speech (TTS) with **dynamic voice selection** filtered by language.
- **PWA Ready**: Installable on mobile devices with full offline support via Service Workers.

## 🛠️ Built With
- **HTML5 / CSS3**: Featuring custom animations (Deep Space Rocket, Atomic Tunnel) and responsive `vmin` design.
- **Vanilla JavaScript**: High-precision mathematical logic using `BigInt` for numbers up to $2^{64}-1$.
- **Web Speech API**: Real-time narration of story milestones and scientific unit transitions.
- **Single Source of Truth Architecture**: Global icon configuration to ensure visual consistency across all languages.
