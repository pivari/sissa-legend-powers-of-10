# Sissa's Legend & Powers of 10

An interactive STEM experience exploring the staggering power of exponential growth, from the ancient Indian Legend of Sissa to the vast scales of the universe and the heart of the atom.

## 🚀 Features
- **Interactive Storytelling**: Experience the "Wheat and Chessboard" problem firsthand.
- **Powers of 10 Journey**: Travel from the macro scale of galaxies down to the micro scale of subatomic particles.
- **PWA Ready**: Installable on mobile devices as a Progressive Web App with offline support.
- **Multilingual Support**: Full English and Italian support with integrated Text-to-Speech (TTS) narration.

## 🛠️ Built With
- **HTML5 / CSS3**: Custom animations and responsive design.
- **Vanilla JavaScript**: Logic using `BigInt` for astronomical calculations.
- **Web Speech API**: Integrated voice narration.
- **Service Workers**: For PWA functionality and offline access.
